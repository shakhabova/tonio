Sending Money to a Bank Account

  • To send money with Remiton to a bank account, log in to your account on our website or mobile app and initiate a new transaction.
  • Select the option to send money to a bank account, and enter the recipient's name, contact information, and bank account details.
  • Choose the amount of money you want to send, and select the payment method you will be using (e.g. bank account, payment card, cash).
  • Review and confirm the details of your transaction, and complete the payment to initiate the transfer.
  • Your recipient will receive a notification or email when the funds are available in their bank account.
