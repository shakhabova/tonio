Sending Money to a Cash Payout Point

  • To send money with Remiton to a cash payout point, log in to your account on our website or mobile app and initiate a new transaction.
  • Select the option to send money to a cash payout point, and enter the recipient's name and contact information.
  • Choose the amount of money you want to send, and select the payment method you will be using (e.g. bank account, payment card, cash).
  • Review and confirm the details of your transaction, and complete the payment to initiate the transfer.
  • Your recipient will receive a notification or email when the funds are available for them to pick up at a designated cash payout point.
